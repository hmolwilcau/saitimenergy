Piensa Solar

Its objective is the monitoring and performance analysis of photovoltaic plants. It captures data from all devices to view their status, make graphic comparisons, manage alarms, and schedule maintenance, all accessible from a mobile device as well.

Comprehensive Control

It allows monitoring overall and detailed performance (strings, inverters) through parameters adjustable by the administrator.

Analysis and Export

Offers advanced tools to view graphical trends and export data in standard formats such as XML and CSV.

Maintenance Management

The system schedules and manages both preventive and corrective maintenance activities, keeping a detailed record of the plant's health.

Mobility

It has an iOS and Android app that makes it easy to track the plant in real time from anywhere.
