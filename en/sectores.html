Renewable Energies

SAITIM was a pioneering company nationwide in the development of monitoring systems for photovoltaic plants since we bet on this sector in 2007. Currently, SAITIM S.L. has its "PIENSA SOLAR" system implemented in more than 150 MW distributed in 30 projects on the European and American continents (Spain, Germany, Italy, France, Slovakia, Ukraine, United Kingdom, Portugal, and Brazil).

Ceramics and Aggregates

The ceramics and aggregates sector is another business area where SAITIM has developed programming for their total automation, primarily brick and tile manufacturing plants and clay milling. In this business area, we have plants in service in Spain, Algeria, Colombia, and Venezuela. In the aggregates sector, we work for our clients performing the control of mortar and concrete plants based on PLC and commercial SCADA.

Automotive

In the automotive industry, we carry out work such as: automation of production lines; software development for production, traceability, and quality; electrical and programming maintenance.

Lifting and Capital Goods

Our industrial experience allows us to develop electrical projects for any type of machinery or application, no matter how complex it may be. The most symbolic and notable projects in this area range from two orbital cranes at the CELLS scientific complex to various coke cranes for refining plants in Turkey, Mexico, or the UAE.

We design, build, and support projects and applications for clients worldwide.
