Piensa Traceability System

It facilitates quick diagnosis and deep troubleshooting. Process management, from the moment raw materials enter the factory to the final product at the farm, represents effective manufacturing control with the aim of solving any issue as efficiently as possible.

Bidirectional control: from field to farm

The system offers absolute control from the field to the farm, recording the entire journey. Towards the supplier, it keeps the history of raw materials, and towards the customer, it details the composition and final destination of the product.

Direct and seamless connection with machinery (PLC)

The software links efficiently with automation (PLC) without using intermediary servers. This allows instant recording of errors or alarms, facilitating extremely fast diagnosis and incident resolution.

Modular management to minimize manufacturing errors

The system divides manufacturing into modules that control everything from barcode reception to bagging. By automating transfers, recipes, and generating independent batches, the risks of human error in the plant are greatly minimized.

Integrated business intelligence and quality control

The software integrates intuitive graphical tools to analyze real performance and availability metrics (OEE). In addition, it connects with laboratory equipment to automatically record the variables of the analyzed samples.
